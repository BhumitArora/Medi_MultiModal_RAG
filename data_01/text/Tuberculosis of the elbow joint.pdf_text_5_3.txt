The aim of this case report is, therefore, to raise aware-
ness of TB affecting peripheral joints, as well as to give 
a clinical recommendation in synopsis with other inter-
national reports. TB of the elbow often presents with 
nonspecific symptoms of a painful joint, sometimes with 
swelling and hyperthermia, accompanied by limited 
range of motion [5, 15, 16].
